Instructions

  1. There are 3 types of QR Codes with different use cases.
  2. Go to this website https://qrexplore.com/generate/ to generate QR Codes.
  3. For every type, click "Copy to Clipboard" and paste it on the website.
  4. On the website, please also check "Treat as CSV" and "Add filename to image".
  5. Click "Generate Codes" and save the QR Codes.

Type 1

Print this type of QR Code on every guest's wedding invitation. When each guests present with their wedding invitation, you can use app to immediately scan and input their money gift.

Type 2

Scan this type of QR Code when a guest forgets to bring his invitation card. Once scanned, type in their name and money gift.

Type 3

Scan this type of QR Code to view current gift amount in mobile phone
